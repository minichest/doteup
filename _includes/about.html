The Start

It all started around 2010, was only around 5 at this point but yeah sky blue has been in my blood for a while. it was kinda around the time i started to speak crazily enough as well as my first steps into school. life could have turned out very different after all and i have no regrets on where my loyalties would lie.

First EPL

6-1. agueroooooooooooooooooo! overall, one of my favourite seasons....it was very much action packed and intriguing. the feeling of beating your rivals to your first ever newly formed league title is a high that will never ever be topped...especially when it's on goal difference.
